## LONG VERSION (For After Hired)

### Service Tiers

**Pilot / Discovery**
- 50-200 leads
- Proves ICP fit + data quality

**Standard Build**
- 1k-10k leads
- Sourcing + enrichment + dedupe

**Automation / Ongoing**
- Scheduled daily/weekly pulls
- Enrichment + delivery
- Zapier / webhook automation

### Deliverables

- CSV dataset with fields:
  - Company
  - Domain
  - First/last name
  - Title
  - Seniority
  - Role category
  - Email
  - Phone
  - LinkedIn
  - Source
  - Confidence score
  - Verification status
- Google Sheet or Airtable database
- QA summary (dedupe rate, verification rate)

### Process

1. **Discovery** - Define ICP (industry, size, geo, titles)
2. **Sourcing** - Collect via APIs/databases
3. **Enrichment + Verification** - Email validation, domain checks
4. **Normalization + Deduplication** - Unify titles, standardize companies
5. **Test Delivery** - Client reviews pilot
6. **Automation / Handoff** - Scheduled pulls or scripts

### Tools

LinkedIn Sales Navigator, ZoomInfo, Clearbit, Hunter.io, Google Sheets, Airtable, Zapier

### Data Quality

- Lead source recorded
- GDPR/CAN-SPAM compliance awareness
- Email verification metrics
- Bounce/spam risk estimate

### Pricing

- Per-lead (tiered by verification)
- Fixed project (ex: 2k validated leads)
- Monthly subscription for ongoing lists
- Add-ons: phone verification, CRM import

### Common Mistakes (Your Advantage)

- Role/title normalization
- Lead source transparency
- Pilot testing before full run
- CRM-ready files

### Onboarding Questions

- Target industries?
- Target job titles?
- Company size/revenue?
- Geography?
- Outreach channels?
- CRM format required?
- Budget for paid data sources?
