### FAQ

**Q: What's a typical reply rate?**
A: 10-25% depending on ICP, offer, and list quality. I optimize for replies, not just opens.

**Q: How do you ensure deliverability?**
A: I set up proper authentication (SPF/DKIM/DMARC), use dedicated sending domains, warm up inboxes, and monitor reputation. Average inbox placement: 95%+.

**Q: Can you also source the leads?**
A: Yes - I can build verified prospect lists or work with your existing list.

**Q: What happens if emails bounce?**
A: Automatic bounce handling, list cleaning, and replacement for hard bounces.

**Q: Is this compliant?**
A: Yes - CAN-SPAM compliant for US, GDPR-aware for EU, with proper unsubscribe handling.

**Q: Do you handle replies?**
A: I can set up auto-responses and reply routing, but typically you handle prospect responses directly.
