### FAQ

**Q: Which platforms should I be on?**
A: Based on your audience, I'll recommend the best platforms. Not every business needs TikTok - LinkedIn might be better for B2B.

**Q: How often should I post?**
A: Quality over quantity. 3-4 high-quality posts per week beats 7 mediocre ones.

**Q: How do you measure ROI?**
A: I track conversions, leads, and revenue attributed to social - not just vanity metrics.

**Q: Can you create video content?**
A: Yes - I create short-form video, graphics, and can edit existing footage.

**Q: Do you handle paid ads?**
A: Yes - I manage Meta, TikTok, LinkedIn, and Twitter ads.
